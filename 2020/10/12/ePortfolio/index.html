How to write summary

  • Closing sentence
  1. My skills include __ and __

What are the three parts of your ePortfolio summary of capabilities?

  1. Introduction
  2. Accomplishment
  3. Skills

Useful adjective for description

  • You are kind: kind/caring/compassionate

  • You work hard: committed/dedicated/responsible/motivated

  • You are honest: trustworthy/honest

  • You work well with others: cooperative
  • You can create things or ideas: creative/innovative
  • You can do the work: confident/self-assured/capable/assertive
  • You can communicate well: articulate/well-spoken

Useful verbs for summary

  • Verbs for creating: created/produced/designed/built/developed/prepared

  • Verbs for researching: researched/analyzed/examined or sometimes tested

  • Verbs for managing: managed/supervised/oversaw

  • Verbs for working: worked on/completed

  • Verbs for presenting: gave/presented

  • Verbs for teaching: taught/educated/trained

  • Verbs for writing: wrote/published/composed/authored
